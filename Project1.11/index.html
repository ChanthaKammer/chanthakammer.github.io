Frequently Asked Questions

Why Elite Coaching?

Our coach has over 10,000 hours playing FPS games alone. From Modern Warfare: Search and Destroy, to Counter Strike Global Offensive.

Who's the coach?

My name is Chantha! I have been playing FPS games for over 10 years. My first favorite videogame was Minecraft. I first got it back in 2010 when Notch was only charging $10 for a copy! The gaming industry since then has grown into something much more than I'm sure any of the early game creators imagined. Currently my favorite game to play is Counter Strike: Source, but only surfing. Surfing has been a hobbie of mine for 6 years, and I first started in Team Fortress 2.

Why are you offering coaching?

I remember first starting Search and Destroy in Modern Warfare 2019 and my friends making fun of me for being bad. So I played tens of hours of it alone while offline in order to catch up. Along the way I met many talented players that played about the skill level that my friends had. Thanks to skill-based matchmaking I was always playing with players that rivaled many of the top COD League players. I got great, and I imagine had I played only with my friends, I wouldn't have become so good. If you are just as much of an introvert as I am, it's hard to reach out to other players in the lobby to ask to play with them. That's why I'm here.

What teaching methods do you use?

Surprisingly, meditation is one of the methods I used to increase my skill at such a rapid pace. If you've ever heard of the flow state, I trained myself to be in it while I was playing. It is important to shut out whatever thoughts you have in your head and focus on the present moment. Whether it takes 15 minutes, or 15 weeks, I want you to become the best version of yourself on the battlefield.
