The Avatar

The moon is the best thing that has ever happened to us. After the Fire Nation attacked, the Avatar had to look to the moon to re-gain all of his power throughout his life, The Avatar always looked to the moon as not only his source of power, but his God.

Earth Nation

Earth Nation

The Earth Nation is the most down-to-earth nation in the world. They also draw their power from the moon, but can pull their power from the ground.

Space Nation

Space Nation

Even though they reside on Earth, the Space Nation draws all of their power from the moon. Comparable to vampires, they can only utilize their powers during the night or when the Moon is visible.

Water Nation

Water Nation

The Water Nation draws their power mostly from water. There have been multiple scientific experiments on their relationship to the moon, but the only source of their power is from water.

Tree Nation

Tree Nation

The Tree Nation relies heavily on the power of the Moon. They are able to manipulate any non-sentient nature such as flowers and trees. They are the best nation at defending because they can grow plants at rapid pace.
